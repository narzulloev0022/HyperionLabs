# Brand

## Positioning

Hyperion Labs is an AI lab building world-class products for Central Asia.

## Vision Statement

Building world-class AI products for Central Asia.

## Brand Attributes

- Futuristic
- Luxurious
- Technical
- Confident
- Human-centered

## Visual Direction

- Primary background: deep space tones
- Accent color: `#4AA391`
- Preferred contrast: dark surfaces with refined teal highlights
- References: Apple, OpenAI, Anthropic, zypl.ai

## Tone of Voice

- Clear and intelligent
- Premium, not loud
- Optimistic but credible
- Global quality with local understanding

## Messaging Principles

- Lead with mission and usefulness
- Avoid generic AI buzzwords
- Emphasize trust, precision, and infrastructure thinking
- Speak as a category-defining company, not a prototype studio
